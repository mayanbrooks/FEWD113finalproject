They Say There's No Such Thing As a Stupid Question...

How Does REVEL work?

REVEL allows our users to have the ultimate VIP experience. Our venue partners carefully curate their perfect list and add you as a VIP member allowing you exclusive access to perks and parties.

Does REVEL cost any money?

REVEL is 100% free to use.

Where can I use REVEL?

Right now, REVEL is only available to use in New York City. We are working on expanding to other key cities soon!

What if I don't want to be included on a list anymore?

If no longer want VIP access to a certain club, go into your membership menu, select the club, and hit "dismiss access."

Where can I see the lists, I've been added to?

In the user menu, click on the button that says Memberships.

Is there a minimum age requirement to use REVEL?

You must be 21+ to use this service. This is required for the safety of our users and venues.

Am I Guaranteed entry into the club, if I am a VIP member on REVEL?

We created REVEL to give exclusive first access to our users. Our partnerships with clubs allow us to give our users a number of benefits. However, entry into a venue is ultimately up to the discretion of the doorman. We cannot guarantee access. If you experience a problem while using our service, please leave us an inquiry on the Contact Us page.
